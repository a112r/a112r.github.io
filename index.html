About me

I am a third-year electrical engineering student at the University of British Columbia. I am also a member and Electrical team lead of UBC Subbots, an engineering design team whose goal is to design and build autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) for the annual Robosub competition in California. I am also serving as the Vice President - Student life on the Electrical and Computer Engineering student society at UBC and treasuerer for the UBC Calisthenics club. Outside of school, I have several hobbies, such as working with electronics, programming and exploring the outdoors. I have done numerous projects as part of these hobbies, some of which can be found on this website.
